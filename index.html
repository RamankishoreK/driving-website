driving school🚙

A driving school is an educational institution that provides instruction and training for individuals seeking to obtain a driver's license or improve their driving skills.
These schools offer a structured and comprehensive program that includes both theoretical knowledge and practical driving experience.

Driving schools typically provide classroom sessions where students learn about traffic rules, road signs, and other essential theoretical aspects of driving.
In some regions, completion of a certain number of classroom hours may be a requirement for obtaining a driver's license.
